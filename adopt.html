How You Can Adopt

If you are interested in adopting any of our available foster(s), please follow the steps outlined below

  1. Reach out to Amber @ Saving Grace Rescue
  2. Schedule a meet and greet
  3. Download and complete the adoption agreement
  4. Return the completed adoption agreement, along with a copy of your driver's license
  5. Send adoption fee to Amber @ Saving Grace Rescue

Adoption Fees: $125 for one kitten $200 for two kittens
